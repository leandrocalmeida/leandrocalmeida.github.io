Leandro Almeida is Computer Network Professor at Federal Institute of Paraíba (IFPB) and Ph.D. student at Federal University of São Carlos (UFSCar). He has a master's degree in Informatics at the Federal University of Paraíba (UFPB). In addition, he is also an official instructor for the Cisco Networking Academy Program and Security Information Specialist.
