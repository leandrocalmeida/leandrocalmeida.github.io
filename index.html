Email: leandro dot almeida at ifpb dot edu dot br
CV: Lattes (Portuguese) Linkedin: Linkedin

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Networks from the Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of Paraíba - IFPB (2007), a Master's degree in Informatics from the Federal University of Paraíba (2013), and a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Federal University of São Carlos (2024), with a sandwich period at the University of Amsterdam (Netherlands). Currently, I am a professor at the Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of Paraíba - IFPB. My expertise lies in the field of Computer Science, particularly in Computer Networks, with a focus on routing, security, machine learning, and network programmability.
